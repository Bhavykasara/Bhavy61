About my hobby

Cricket is my favourite hobby because I like this game a lot and I like to play it. It is really a interesting game and also increases our physical fittness. I like this game also because it is a multiplayer game of 22 player 11 players in each team and I like multiplayer games. I like to bat as I am a batsman and now I have started playing some tournaments so now my interest is at the highest level as always. To get better experience I go to cricket classes and I have been going to cricket classes since to three years and right now because of pandemic corona virus (COVID-19) our cricket classes are closed but nothing can keep me away from cricket so I practise cricket at home. I like many other things like studies, coding and many other things but I like cricket the most because cricket is my favourite game and I after playing some cricket I feel relaxed and then I can do anything with my heart. That is why cricket is my favourite hobby.
